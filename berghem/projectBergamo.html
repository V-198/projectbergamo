Bergamo

Where is it?

Bergamo is a city in the north part of Italy, approximatly 40 km from Milan, 30 from Lake Como and Lake Iseo.

Most famous places to visit

  • Cittadella
  • Piazza Vecchia
  • Basilica di Santa Maria Maggiore
  • GAMeC

Education

  • Bergamo University
  • Mario negri institute for pharmacological research
  • Accademia Carrara
